Problem Statement 1

                                                                                “Budgeting has only one rule: Do not go over budget.”

New York state has the largest municipal budget in the United States. Funds are set aside to operate each city agency. This also includes the Office of the Mayor, Board of Election, Law Enforcement Departments such as Police, Fire and Law department etc. According to Forbes, The Fire Department (NYFD) payroll included 18,679 employees in the year 2019, 8,970 of whom made six-figures or more.
